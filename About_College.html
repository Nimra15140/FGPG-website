The Founder Principal

Mrs Anis Zaidi

Mrs Anis Zaidi, a great mind and a great soul envisaged academic development for the womenfolk of Wah Cantt..

F.G. Postgraduate College for Women, Wah Cantt, had a modest beginning as an educational institution in 1954when it appeared on the scene as a Primary School.

Started as an institution in 1954 as a Primary School

Primary school converted in High School

Intermediate College in 1956

College to Degree level in 1967

Came under the Federal Government in 1975

In 2004 M.A/ M.Sc classses was introduced.
