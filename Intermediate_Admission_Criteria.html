Admission Criteria

1.No Re-Admission to first year will be allowed except in cases where a student could not appear in the first-year examinations owing to medical reasons. Prior information in this respect will be given to the college.

    Fee Rules/ Structure

    1. Fee once paid is not refundable.

    2. All official receipts must be kept in safe custody because they may be checked anytime.

    3. College leaving certificate is issued only after all dues have been paid. A student is expected to have paid fee up to the month during which CLC is issued. A student is required to collect security fee while taking the CLC.

    4. A student is required to inform the college immediately if she decides to discontinue studies, failing which, she will continue to run in arrears of fee which will have to be cleared befor CLC is issued and the security deposit refunded.

    5. The security deposit shall stand lapsed, in favour of the college after one year of declaration of final examination results.

    Payment Of fee/ Fine

    Until her name is formally withdrawn, a student is liable to pay the college fee and fines levied upon her. A student absenting herself without leave application for more then six working days is liable to have her name struck off from the college roll.

    Fee collection Dates

    Fee challan is issued by the college office and deposited in the bank at the time of admission. Fee is collected in advance for all disciplines.

    Fee Concession

    1.The Principle has the power to remit only half of the tuition fee of a limited number of deserving student.

    2.Good conduct and satisfactory progress in studies are assential requirements for the grant of financial assistance.

    3.Fee concession may be withdrawn for misconduct irregularity and poor academic performance.

    4.Half tutition fee concession is must be made on the prescribed from available from the college office and it must be countersigne by the father/mother of the application and duty verified by his/her employer in respect of monthly emolument.
